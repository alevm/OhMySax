About the Shop

At OhMySax, we specialize in the repair, restoration, and sale of vintage saxophones. Every horn that passes through our hands is treated with the respect and craftsmanship it deserves. Whether it's a dent, a pad job, or a full overhaul — we bring these beautiful instruments back to their former glory.

Our Services

♫

Full Overhaul

Complete disassembly, cleaning, new pads, corks, felts, and regulation. Your horn comes back like new.

⚙

Dent Removal

Careful dent work on bodies, bells, and necks. We preserve the original lacquer and engraving.

♬

Pad Replacement

Individual pad work or full re-pad using premium leather pads for the best seal and tone.

✿

Custom Engraving

Restore faded engravings or add custom artwork to make your saxophone truly one of a kind.
